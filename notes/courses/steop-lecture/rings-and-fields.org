* Definitions
- Rings :: is a mathematical set equipped with two binary operations, most commonly reffered to as addition and multipllication. These operations bust follow several specific properties for a collection of elements to qualify as a ring. Important concept related to rings include commutativity, associativity, identity elements, distributive laws. and the existence of opposites.
