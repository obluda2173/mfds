* Problem 1 :taylor_polynomial:multivariable_calculus:
** Task
#+begin_quote
Let $f : (-\frac{1}{2}, \infty) \times (-\frac{1}{2}, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}$ be defined by

\[ f(x, y) = \log(1 + x + y) \]

(a) Determine the Taylor polynomial of order 3 of $f$ at $(0, 0)$.
(b) Use your result to approximate $f(0.1, 0.1)$.
(c) Plot heatmaps of $f$, of the Taylor polynomial of order 3, and of the difference of the two in $[0, 1]^2$.
#+end_quote

** Solution

*** (a)

\begin{align*}
f(x) &= \sum_{|\alpha| \le K} \frac{\partial^{\alpha} f(x_0)}{\alpha!} (x - x_0)^{\alpha} + o(\|x - x_0\|^{K})
\end{align*}

Since $f(x,y) = \log(1 + x + y)$ depends on $(x,y)$ only through $s = x + y$, all partials of equal total order coincide:

\begin{align*}
\partial_1 f &= \partial_2 f = \dfrac{1}{1 + x + y} \\
\partial_1^2 f &= \partial_1 \partial_2 f = \partial_2^2 f = -\dfrac{1}{(1 + x + y)^2} \\
\partial_1^3 f &= \partial_1^2 \partial_2 f = \partial_1 \partial_2^2 f = \partial_2^3 f = \dfrac{2}{(1 + x + y)^3} \\
\end{align*}

Evaluated at $(0,0)$:

\begin{align*}
f(0,0) &= 0 \\
\partial_1 f(0,0) &= 1 \\
\partial_1^2 f(0,0) &= -1 \\
\partial_1^3 f(0,0) &= 2 \\
\end{align*}

Each term of $P_3$ has the form

$$\mathrm{term}_{\alpha} = \dfrac{\partial^{\alpha} f(0,0)}{\alpha!} x^{\alpha_1} y^{\alpha_2}.$$

Order $0$: $\alpha = (0,0)$, term $= 0$.

Order $1$:

- $\alpha = (1,0)$: $\dfrac{1}{1!\,0!}\, x = x$
- $\alpha = (0,1)$: $\dfrac{1}{0!\,1!}\, y = y$

Sum: $x + y$.

Order $2$:

- $\alpha = (2,0)$: $\dfrac{-1}{2!\,0!}\, x^2 = -\dfrac{1}{2}x^2$
- $\alpha = (1,1)$: $\dfrac{-1}{1!\,1!}\, xy = -xy$
- $\alpha = (0,2)$: $\dfrac{-1}{0!\,2!}\, y^2 = -\dfrac{1}{2}y^2$

Sum: $-\dfrac{1}{2}x^2 - xy - \dfrac{1}{2}y^2 = -\dfrac{1}{2}(x+y)^2$.

Order $3$:

- $\alpha = (3,0)$: $\dfrac{2}{3!\,0!}\, x^3 = \dfrac{1}{3}x^3$
- $\alpha = (2,1)$: $\dfrac{2}{2!\,1!}\, x^2 y = x^2 y$
- $\alpha = (1,2)$: $\dfrac{2}{1!\,2!}\, x y^2 = x y^2$
- $\alpha = (0,3)$: $\dfrac{2}{0!\,3!}\, y^3 = \dfrac{1}{3}y^3$

Sum: $\dfrac{1}{3}x^3 + x^2 y + x y^2 + \dfrac{1}{3}y^3 = \dfrac{1}{3}(x+y)^3$.

Hence,

\begin{align*}
P_3(x,y) &= (x + y) - \dfrac{1}{2}(x+y)^2 + \dfrac{1}{3}(x+y)^3.
\end{align*}

*** (b)

\begin{align*}
P_3(0.1, 0.1) &= (0.1+0.1) - \frac{1}{2}(0.1+0.1)^2 + \frac{1}{3}(0.1+0.1)^3 \\
&= (0.2) - \frac{1}{2}(0.2)^2 + \frac{1}{3}(0.2)^3 \\
&\approx 0.1826 \\
\end{align*}

*** (c)

#+begin_src julia :results value
using Plots

f(x, y) = log(1 + x + y)
P3(x, y) = (x + y) - 1/2 * (x + y)^2 + 1/3 * (x + y)^3
err(x, y) = f(x, y) - P3(x, y)

xs = 0:0.01:1
ys = 0:0.01:1

cl = (0, P3(1, 1))

p1 = heatmap(xs, ys, f,
             title="f",
             clim=cl,
             aspect_ratio=1,
             c=:viridis)

p2 = heatmap(xs, ys, P3,
             title="P3",
             clim=cl,
             aspect_ratio=1,
             c=:viridis)

p3 = heatmap(xs, ys, err,
             title="f - P3",
             aspect_ratio=1,
             c=cgrad(:reds, rev=true))

p4 = surface(xs, ys, f,
             title="f vs P3",
             c=:viridis,
             alpha=0.7)

surface!(p4, xs, ys, P3,
         c=:viridis,
         alpha=0.7)

plot(p1, p2, p3, p4, layout=(2,2), size=(1200, 1200))

mkpath("./assets")
savefig("./assets/p_01_c.png")
#+end_src
