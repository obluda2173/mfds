* Problem 4                                        :jacobian:linear_approximation:
** Task
#+begin_quote
Let $f : \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R}^2$ be defined by
\[
f(x, y) = \begin{pmatrix} x y \\ x^2 + y^3 \end{pmatrix}
\]

(a) Compute the Jacobi matrix $J_f(x, y)$.

(b) Evaluate $J_f(1, 1)$.

(c) Determine the (affine) linear approximation of $f$ near the point $(1, 1)$.

(d) Use this approximation to estimate $f(1.01, 0.98)$.
#+end_quote

** Solution

* Errors/Typos
- Problem 2(b): "$A^* A x = b$" → "$A^* A x = A^* b$" (standard normal equations for least squares; as written, the equation is dimensionally correct but does not characterize the minimizer unless $b \in \operatorname{im}(A)$ and additional conditions hold)
