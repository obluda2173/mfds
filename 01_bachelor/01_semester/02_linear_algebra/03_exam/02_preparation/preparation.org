#+begin_quote
a. In this exercise, we work over the field $\mathbb{R}$. Consider
   $A=\begin{bmatrix}2&1&-1\\ 4&5&-3\\ -2&5&-2\end{bmatrix}\in\mathbb{R}^{3\times3}$,
   $b=\begin{bmatrix}1\\ 7\\ 3\end{bmatrix}\in\mathbb{R}^{3}$.

   Perform Gaussian elimination on the system $Ax=b$ without row exchanges. Show explicitly how you eliminate the entries below the diagonal in the first and second columns, and write down the resulting upper-triangular matrix $U$.

b. Let $m_{21}$, $m_{31}$ be the multipliers used to eliminate the entries in the first column, and let $m_{32}$ be the multiplier used to eliminate the $(3, 2)$ -entry after the first elimination step.
   Form the unit lower-triangular matrix

   $$L=\begin{bmatrix}1&0&0\\ m_{21}&1&0\\ m_{31}&m_{32}&1\end{bmatrix}$$

   Verify that your matrices satisfy $A=LU$.

c. Solve $Ax=b$ using the factorization $A=LU$ by (i) solving $Ly=b$ for $y$ (forward substitution) and then (ii) solving $Ux=y$ for $x$ (backward substitution). Present your intermediate vector $y$ and the final solution $x$.

d. (Programming, Julia) Implement two Julia functions =forward_substitution(L,b)= and =backward_substitution(U,y)= that solve $Ly=b$ band $Ux=y$ for triangular matrices of compatible sizes. Use your code to solve the system from this exercise and verify that your result agrees with Julia's $A \setminus b$.
#+end_quote

*** Solution
**** (a)


\begin{align*}
[A \mid b] =
&\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c}
2 & 1 & -1 & 1 \\
4 & 5 & -3 & 7 \\
-2 & 5 & -2 & 3
\end{array}\right] \\
\xrightarrow{m_{21}=2,\; m_{31}=-1}
&\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c}
2 & 1 & -1 & 1 \\

\end{array}\right] \\
\xrightarrow{m_{32}=2}

2 & 1 & -1 & 1 \\
0 & 3 & -1 & 5 \\
0 & 0 & -1 & -6
\end{array}\right]
= [U \mid \tilde{b}]
\end{align*}

function forward_substitution(L, b)
    n = size(L, 1)
    y = zeros(Float64, n)

    for i in 1:n
        sum = b[i]

        for j in 1:(i-1)
            sum -= L[i, j] * y[j]
        end

        y[i] = sum / L[i, i]
    end
    return y
end

function backward_substitution(U, y)
    n = size(U, 1)
    x = zeros(Float64, n)

    for i in n:-1:1
        sum = y[i]

        for j in (i+1):n
            sum -= U[i, j] * x[j]
        end
        x[i] = sum / U[i, i]
    end
    return x
end

L = [ 1  0  0;
      2  1  0;
     -1  2  1]

U = [ 2  1 -1;
      0  3 -1;
      0  0 -1]

b = [ 1;
      7;
      3]

y = forward_substitution(L, b)
x = backward_substitution(U, y)

println("Intermediate vector y: ", y)
println("Final solution x: ", x)
